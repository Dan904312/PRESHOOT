What is PreShoot
Never run out of content ideas.
Point your camera at any location and instantly get 6 ready-to-film video ideas tailored to what you can see.
📸
How it works
Three steps. That's it.
1
Take a photo
Point at wherever you are. A car park, rooftop, gym, street. Anywhere works.
2
Get 6 ideas instantly
PreShoot reads the actual scene and generates ideas specific to what it sees.
3
Go film it
Each idea has a hook, shot guide, audio style and a direct CapCut link.
What you get
Every idea comes fully loaded.
🎣

Word-for-word script for the first 3 seconds
🎥
Shot guide
Exact camera angle and movement
🎵
Audio style
Sound and music direction
