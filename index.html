How it works

From location to filming
in three steps.

No brief. No brief. No creative block. Scan where you are and walk away knowing exactly what to film.

01  Capture
📸

Take a photo

Point your camera at any location — a car park, a rooftop, a gym, a street. PreShoot reads exactly what it sees and builds ideas around it. The more specific the scene, the sharper the ideas.

02  Choose
🎯

Set the format

Pick a video format — Advert, UGC, Viral, Storytelling, Educational, Trial Reel, or Meme. Add any context about your brand or audience. PreShoot locks every idea to that structure.

03  Film
🎬

Walk on set ready

Six ideas, each with a word-for-word hook, camera angle, editing style, audio direction, and YouTube reference. Link straight to CapCut templates. You show up knowing exactly what you're shooting.
