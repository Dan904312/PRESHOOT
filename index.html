How it works

Three steps to your next video

No creative block, no wasted time on set. Just point, scan, and film.

Step 01
📸

Take a photo

Point your camera at wherever you are — a car park, rooftop, gym, street. Any location works. The more detail the AI can see, the better your ideas.

Step 02
✨

Choose your format

Pick a video format — Advert, UGC, Educational, Viral, Storytelling, Trial Reel, or Meme. Add specific context about your brand or audience if needed.

Step 03
🎬

Go film it

Get 6 detailed, location-specific ideas — each with a word-for-word hook, camera guide, editing direction, audio style, and CapCut template link.
