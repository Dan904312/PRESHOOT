Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

Welcome to PreShoot ("PreShoot", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, web application, and related services.

By using PreShoot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you create an account using Google Login, we may collect:

  • Your email address
  • Account creation/sign-up date
  • Basic profile information associated with your Google account

Creator Profile & Usage Data

To personalize your experience, PreShoot may store:

  • Creator profile information (niche, platform, aesthetic, gear)
  • Past scan history
  • Preferences and usage patterns
  • AI-generated outputs and recommendations

This information is used to improve personalization and provide more relevant content suggestions.

Uploaded Content

Users may upload images or other content for AI analysis and content generation purposes.

PreShoot itself does not manually access or review uploaded user content unless required for security, legal compliance, abuse prevention, or technical troubleshooting.

Payment Information

Payments and subscriptions are processed securely through Stripe. PreShoot does not store or directly access full credit card or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate PreShoot services
  • Generate AI-powered content ideas and recommendations
  • Personalize outputs based on your creator profile and scan history
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse of the platform
  • Maintain platform security and stability

3. AI Processing & Third-Party Providers

PreShoot uses third-party AI providers, including Anthropic, to generate responses and content suggestions.

Uploaded content and related prompts may be securely transmitted to these providers in order for features to function properly.

YouTube & CapCut integration. To keep ideas grounded in real, working examples — not just text — PreShoot links each generated idea to relevant YouTube reference videos and CapCut templates. These are generated search links to publicly available content on those platforms; PreShoot does not upload, store, or share your photos or personal data with YouTube or CapCut. Clicking these links takes you to the respective platform under their own terms and privacy policies.

We do not control how third-party providers process data once transmitted through their systems. Users should also review the privacy policies of:

  • Anthropic
  • Stripe
  • Google
  • Vercel
  • Supabase
  • YouTube / Google
  • CapCut / Bytedance

4. Hosting & Infrastructure

PreShoot uses third-party infrastructure and hosting providers, including:

  • Vercel
  • Supabase

These providers may process and store data as necessary to operate the platform securely and reliably.

PreShoot may be accessed by users worldwide. By using the service, you understand that your information may be processed or stored in countries outside your own jurisdiction, including countries that may have different data protection laws.

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us.
